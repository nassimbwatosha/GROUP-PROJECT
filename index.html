About HIV/AIDS

HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) attacks the immune system. With proper treatment, people living with HIV can live long and healthy lives. Awareness helps reduce stigma and prevent new infections.

Problems Addressed

  • Increasing rate of HIV/AIDS patients
  • Stigma against people living with HIV
  • Lack of knowledge about living healthily with HIV
  • Misunderstanding about HIV-free babies from HIV-positive mothers

Prevention

  • Preventative Contraceptives
  • Limit sexual partners
  • Regular HIV testing
  • Avoid sharing needles
  • Use preventive medicine like PrEP
  • Educate communities

Hope Beyond HIV

People living with HIV can lead healthy and productive lives with proper treatment and support. Pregnant mothers with HIV can give birth to HIV-free babies with the right medical care.
